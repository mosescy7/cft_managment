About Us
Lamu Investments Rwanda Ltd is a community-focused investment company based in Kimironko, Kigali, committed to creating modern, mixed-use spaces that enhance everyday life. Through thoughtful development, we bring essential services together under one roof, offering a convenient one-stop hub where people can live, work, dine, and connect. Rooted in community and driven by a vision for sustainable urban growth, Lamu Investments plays an active role in shaping vibrant neighborhoods and supporting local economic development.
